Lemon Pie

This lemon meringue pie is so delicious and perfect for sharing! The sweet and tart lemon filling is thickened with cornstarch, flour, and egg yolks.

Ingredients

    For the filling:
  • White Sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Lemons
  • Buter
  • Beatten Egg Yolks
    • For the Meringue:
  • Egg whites
  • White sugar

Preparation

    Make the filling
  1. Combine the dry ingredients in a saucepan
  2. Add the water and the lemon juice and zest
  3. Bring to boil, then stir in the butter
  4. Place the egg yolks in a bowl and stir in half the sugar mixture.
  5. Whisk this mixture back into the original mixture
    Make the meringue
  1. Beat the eggs whites until foamy
  2. Add the Sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form
    Bake the pie
  1. Pour the filling into the pie shell with meringue
  2. Bake in the preheated oven until the meringue is golden brown
