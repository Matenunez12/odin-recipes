Brownies

Bake these easy brownies whenever you need to quickly satisfy your sweet tooth. Made with kitchen staples, you'll come back to this easy brownie recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts

Preparation

  1. Mix the ingredientes togheter in a bowl.
  2. Spread the batter into a prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry.
  4. Cool, then cut into squares.
